Student Terms and Conditions

Fees & Refunds

Fees are non-transferable. Refund requests are allowed within 7 days of payment OR before the program starts (whichever is earlier). 30% of the total fee paid will be deducted as an administrative charge. No refunds once 7 days have passed or after LMS/course access begins.

Batch Transfers

Transfers are not allowed, unless approved with a transfer fee.

Policies

IBMP may update rules, requirements, or curriculum anytime. Updates will be shared officially.

Your Information

Keep IBMP updated on changes to your address, phone, or email.

Documents

Submit all required certificates/IDs as requested. Admission may stay provisional until documents are verified.

Authenticity

All documents must be genuine. Fake or false documents will cause admission cancellation, fee loss, and possible legal action.

Conduct

Maintain professional and academic integrity. Misconduct or plagiarism may result in suspension or dismissal.

Jurisdiction

All disputes follow the laws of the country where IBMP is incorporated.
